Detailed Description

Quillnova is an AI-powered LinkedIn post generator that helps you create engaging, viral-worthy content in seconds. Whether you're building your personal brand, sharing thought leadership, or telling compelling stories, Quillnova has you covered.


6 Writing Styles: Choose from Thought Leader, Story, How-To, Personal Brand, Hot Take, and Data-Driven styles, each optimized for LinkedIn's algorithm and engagement patterns.


Hook Generator: Generate 10 scroll-stopping opening lines per topic. First impressions matter — make yours count.


Hashtag Engine: Automatically generate 15 relevant hashtags ranked by reach and relevance to maximize your post's discoverability.


Tone Adjustment: Fine-tune your voice with 6 tone options — Professional, Casual, Inspirational, Humorous, Urgent, or Empathetic.


10 Languages: Create content in English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Italian, Hindi, Chinese, Japanese, and Arabic.


LinkedIn Preview: See exactly how your post will appear on LinkedIn before you publish. Pixel-perfect mockup.


Templates & Favorites: 8 pre-built templates for quick starts, plus save up to 50 favorite posts for reuse.
