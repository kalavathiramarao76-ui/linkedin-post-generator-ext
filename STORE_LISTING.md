## Detailed Description

PostCraft AI is the best AI-powered LinkedIn post generator Chrome extension. Write engaging, viral LinkedIn posts in seconds with 6 professional writing styles, smart hooks, hashtag suggestions, tone adjustment, and multi-language support.

### Key Features

- **6 Writing Styles** — Thought Leader, Storyteller, Data-Driven, Motivational, Contrarian, and Tutorial formats
- **AI Hook Generator** — Attention-grabbing opening lines that stop the scroll
- **Smart Hashtag Suggestions** — Relevant, trending hashtags for maximum reach
- **Tone Adjustment** — Professional, casual, inspirational, or bold tone controls
- **Multi-Language Support** — Generate posts in multiple languages
- **Real-Time Streaming** — Watch your post being written in real time
- **One-Click Copy** — Instantly copy generated posts to clipboard
- **Side Panel Workspace** — Full editor with history, drafts, and advanced options
- **Dark & Light Themes** — Comfortable content creation day or night

### How It Works

1. Click the PostCraft AI icon in your Chrome toolbar
2. Enter your topic or idea
3. Select a writing style (Thought Leader, Storyteller, etc.)
4. Click "Generate Post" and watch AI craft your content
5. Copy and paste directly into LinkedIn

### Use Cases

- Content creators building a LinkedIn audience
- Founders and executives sharing thought leadership
- Marketing professionals creating consistent LinkedIn content
- Sales teams using social selling strategies
- Career coaches sharing career advice content
- Anyone who wants to post on LinkedIn but struggles with writing

### Technical Details

- Manifest V3 Chrome extension (latest standard)
- Real-time streaming AI generation
- Works alongside LinkedIn for seamless posting
- No LinkedIn login required
- Lightweight and fast

### Privacy

PostCraft AI sends your topic to our AI API for content generation only. No personal data is collected or stored. Your generated posts are yours — we do not retain or reuse them.
